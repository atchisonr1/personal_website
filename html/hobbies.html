Golfing

In my free time, I absolutely love to play golf. It is starting to look like spring in Northern Kentucky, and I couldn't be happier. I started playing golf in high school, and I played for the girls' team at Paul Laurence Dunbar High School in Lexington, Kentucky. I got the chance to play golf all over the Commonwealth. After I graduated high school, I went on to play golf on the womens' team at Georgetown College for two years. There, I got the chance to play even more courses around the south-eastern side of the United States. On the right, you'll see a picture of me playing in a golf tournament in Florida. Below, I have a few of my favorite courses listed (in no particular order).

  • Dale Hollow Lake State Park Golf Course in Burkesville, Kentucky
  • Stonecrest Golf Course in Prestonsburg, Kentucky
  • Kearney Hill Golf Links in Lexington, Kentucky
  • Connemara Golf Course in Nicholasville, Kentucky
