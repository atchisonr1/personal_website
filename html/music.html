Pop Punk

Throughout my life, I have been exposed to many music genres. Pop Punk continues to be my favorite genre, with 80's Rock trailing close behind. I look back to when I was younger and I remember hearing Bowling for Soup's "1985" and Blink-182's "All the Small Things" on the radio. Somehow, through all these years, these are still the songs and the musical groups that I listen to today. I like listening to Pop Punk because it is (usually) energetic, fun, and it helps me de-stress. Below, I have an ordered list of my favorite Pop Punk bands and some of my favorite songs.

  1. Blink-182
    1. What's my Age Again?
    2. Go
    3. Down
  2. +44
    1. No, It Isn't
    2. Cliff Diving
    3. Lillian
  3. Bowling for Soup
    1. 1985
    2. Almost
    3. Everything to Me

80's Rock

I enjoy listening to 80's Rock because it is wild, exciting, and it reminds me of my childhood. Below, I have an ordered list of my favorite 80's Rock bands and some of my favorite songs.
